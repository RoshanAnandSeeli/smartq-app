Key Features

Built for modern waiting management

The prototype blends queue operations, customer communication, and lightweight engagement into a single system that feels practical and scalable.

01

Digital token generation

Customers no longer need to stand in a physical line. The system assigns tokens digitally and keeps everyone in an organized sequence.

02

Admin queue control

The admin panel shows the current token, queue count, service timing, customer satisfaction status, and quick controls to move the queue forward.

03

Real-time ETA visibility

Customers can track how many minutes remain and see exactly which token is being served, reducing uncertainty and crowd pressure.

04

WhatsApp integration

Customers first connect to the Twilio sandbox with join spider-heavy sent to +1 (415) 523-8886. After that, they send hi, link their queue and token, and use status or ai <message> for updates.

05

AI-driven assistance

The built-in assistant keeps waiting customers informed with status messaging, encouragement, and lightweight interaction while they wait.

06

Engagement and reward layer

A mini-game and discount logic help turn waiting time into a better customer experience while giving the admin more flexibility when queues slow down.

Why this matters

Traditional queues waste time and create frustration. Smart Queue Management improves service efficiency, lowers crowding, and gives customers a more predictable experience, which makes it relevant for hospitals, banks, colleges, offices, and service centers.

Entrepreneurial value

This idea has product potential as a low-cost service platform for local businesses and institutions that want better customer handling without complex hardware.
